Fashion design is the art of creating clothing and accessories.
As an academic field of study, fashion design pulls from many
disciplines, including history, business, illustration, and technology.
As an art practice, fashion design is intertwined with culture.
Fashion design is the art of applying design, aesthetics, clothing construction and natural beauty to clothing and its accessories.
Fashion designers sketch designs of clothing, footwear, and accessories.
Fashion designers create original clothing, accessories, and footwear.
They sketch designs, select fabrics and patterns, and
give instructions on how to make the products they design.
Fashion Design is amongst the highest-paying industries,
offers lucrative job opportunities and is also a creatively
fulfilling and financially rewarding career!
It is influenced by culture and different trends, and
has varied over time and place.
